About Me

I'm Kenneth Malinao, a passionate Front-end Developer and UI Designer based in the Philippines. I craft clean, performant, and visually compelling digital experiences that bridge the gap between great design and solid engineering.

With a sharp eye for detail and a love for modern web aesthetics, I specialize in building responsive interfaces using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and modern frameworks — making sure every pixel feels intentional and every interaction feels smooth.

When I'm not pushing pixels, I'm exploring new design systems, contributing to open-source projects, and leveling up my skills in UI motion and interactive storytelling.
